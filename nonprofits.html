Nonprofits We Support

Highlighting the community organizations partnered with UT students.

Partnership Focus Areas

We collaborate with nonprofits focused on education equity, food security, youth mentorship, and health access. Each partnership begins with a needs assessment so that student volunteers can deliver targeted, high-impact support.

How We Work Together

Nonprofit partners gain dedicated student teams, data-informed recommendations, and ongoing communication. We aim to co-create initiatives that are both practical and measurable.
