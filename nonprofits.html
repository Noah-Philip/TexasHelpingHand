Service Model

How partner needs connect to our three engagement modes.

Across every mission area, Texas Helping Hand matches students to the kind of support nonprofits need most: hands-on service, project-based problem solving, or community advocacy grounded in research.

Volunteer

Best for direct service needs like food sorting, shelter support, donation drives, outreach shifts, and event staffing.

Consulting support

Best for operational and strategic needs like data analysis, website updates, communications plans, and process improvements.

Advocacy/research

Best for awareness campaigns, policy research, speaker events, trend analysis, and materials that amplify partner voices.
