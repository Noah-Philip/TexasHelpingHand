About Texas Helping Hand

Learn how UT students are partnering with nonprofits across Texas.

Who We Are

Texas Helping Hand is a student-led organization at UT that matches nonprofit partners with talented students ready to contribute time, research, and strategic support. Our leadership team brings together majors from across campus to deliver thoughtful, community-centered engagement.

What We Value

We prioritize transparency, mutual respect, and sustainable solutions. Every partnership is designed to meet real community needs while giving students a meaningful opportunity to learn through service.

Texas Helping Hand

We are a registered UT student organization and are not affiliated with The University of Texas at Austin.
