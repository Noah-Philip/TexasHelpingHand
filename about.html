Our Story

Built by students. Grounded in Austin. Focused on practical care.

Texas Helping Hand is a UT student organization that collaborates with nonprofit teams to expand service capacity where community needs are urgent and ongoing.

"Our goal is consistency: reliable student volunteers, reliable follow-through, and reliable support for every partner."

— Executive leadership team

Who we are

Cross-campus students committed to community service.

Our team brings together students from public policy, business, social work, health sciences, and engineering to provide thoughtful support to nonprofit operations and outreach.

What we value

Partnership over one-time service.

We center transparency, mutual respect, and sustainable planning so nonprofit leaders can trust our teams to show up and contribute in meaningful ways.

Leadership

Executive Team

  • President: Leads strategy, nonprofit partnerships, and campus collaboration.
  • Vice President: Oversees volunteer operations and service planning.
  • Director of Outreach: Manages community engagement and member recruitment.
  • Director of Partnerships: Coordinates communication with nonprofit partners.
  • Treasurer: Supervises fundraising efforts and financial stewardship.
