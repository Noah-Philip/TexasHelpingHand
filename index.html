Who We Are

More than a student org.

Texas Helping Hand is a UT student organization dedicated to tackling homelessness in Austin through direct action, strategic consulting, and meaningful advocacy.

We believe every student has the power to create real, lasting change in their community. Whether you're passionate about service, strategy, or policy, there is a place for you here.

What We Do

Three ways we fight homelessness.

01

Volunteer

Hands-on service supporting communities in need across Austin. We show up every week and work alongside our nonprofit partners.

  • Weekly volunteer opportunities
  • Food and supply drives
  • Care package assembly
  • Shelter support shifts
  • Community conferences
Take Action
02

Consult

We partner with local nonprofits to build data-driven solutions that create measurable community impact long after a single event ends.

  • Data analysis projects
  • Website revamps and SEO
  • Legislator analysis
  • Policy presentations
  • Social media strategy
Make Impact
03

Advocate

We amplify voices and push for policy change through research, awareness campaigns, and direct engagement with community leaders.

  • Legislator outreach
  • Speaker events
  • Awareness campaigns
  • Research briefs
  • Social media projects
