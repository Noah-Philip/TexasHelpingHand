Our Mission

We mobilize UT students to deliver sustainable, hands-on assistance for nonprofits that uplift Texas communities. By pairing student talent with nonprofit needs, we create measurable outcomes, strengthen local partnerships, and inspire lifelong civic leadership.

“When students and nonprofits collaborate, we turn campus energy into community change.”

About Us

Texas Helping Hand is a student-led initiative rooted at The University of Texas at Austin. We design service programs that connect students with nonprofit partners focused on homelessness, food insecurity, and navigation services. Our teams coordinate volunteer placement, donation drives, and educational events throughout the academic year.

Executive Members

  • President: Leads strategy, nonprofit partnerships, and campus collaboration.
  • Vice President: Oversees volunteer operations and service planning.
  • Director of Outreach: Manages community engagement and member recruitment.
  • Director of Partnerships: Coordinates communication with nonprofit partners.
  • Treasurer: Supervises fundraising efforts and financial stewardship.
