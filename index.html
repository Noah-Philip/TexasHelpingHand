Helping restore dignity, stability, and hope

Texas Helping Hand brings together UT students, community partners, and local leaders to support neighbors experiencing homelessness and housing instability. Through direct service, coordinated outreach, and long-term partnership, we turn compassion into practical action and help create pathways toward safety, support, and lasting stability.

Our Mission

We mobilize UT students to deliver sustainable, hands-on assistance for nonprofits that uplift Texas communities. By pairing student talent with nonprofit needs, we create measurable outcomes, strengthen local partnerships, and inspire lifelong civic leadership.

“When students and nonprofits collaborate, we turn campus energy into community change.”
